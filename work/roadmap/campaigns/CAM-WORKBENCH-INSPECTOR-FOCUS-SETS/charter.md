# CAM-WORKBENCH-INSPECTOR-FOCUS-SETS

## Objective

Add a reusable focus-set workflow for design tuning by letting developers mark important semantic targets and hide unrelated branches.

## In scope

- mark and unmark targets from the tree and selected-target panel
- focus-only filtering
- local dev-only persistence for focus and hierarchy preferences

## Out of scope

- shared presets
- backend persistence
- automatic control generation
